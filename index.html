How we work

A simple path from data to decisions

Every engagement follows the same disciplined arc — whether it's a three-month dashboard build or a full transformation roadmap.

01

Diagnose

We start with your data, systems, and processes as they are today — identifying gaps, bottlenecks, and quick wins.

02

Design

We design dashboards, workflows, or training plans tailored to your team's tools, skills, and goals — not a one-size-fits-all template.

03

Deploy

We build and roll out the solution alongside your team, with hands-on support so adoption sticks from day one.

04

Develop

Through iAnalyse mentorship and ongoing advisory, we keep building your team's capacity long after launch.
